About VÉRAÉ

VÉRAÉ is a next-generation skincare brand designed for the modern world. We understand that your skin doesn't live in a bubble — it's constantly exposed to environmental factors like pollution, humidity, temperature, and more. That's why we go beyond generic skincare. At VÉRAÉ, we combine clean ingredients with intelligent technology to recommend products tailored to your local environment. Whether you're dealing with urban pollution, dry air, or seasonal shifts, our smart system helps you discover what your skin truly needs. No location data? No problem. Our formulas are effective even without customization, offering simple yet powerful solutions for healthy, glowing skin.

HERITAGE

Rooted in the rich traditions of Indian skincare, VÉRAÉ blends time-tested natural wisdom with modern science. Inspired by nature, powered by technology — we honour the past while creating the future of skincare.

Our Story

At VÉRAÉ, our journey began with a simple belief — skincare should adapt to you, not the other way around. Living in cities with rising pollution and unpredictable weather, we realized that traditional skincare doesn't consider the real world your skin faces daily. That's why we created VÉRAÉ: a blend of clean ingredients and intelligent technology that understands your environment and recommends what your skin actually needs. From a small idea to a growing skincare movement, we're here to make personalized, effective skincare simple — wherever you are.
